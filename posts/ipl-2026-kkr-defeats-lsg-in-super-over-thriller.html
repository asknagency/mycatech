IPL 2026: KKR Defeats LSG in Super Over Thriller

Published on 27/04/2026

The IPL 2026 season delivered an absolute classic last night at the Ekana Cricket Stadium in Lucknow. The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) managed to defeat the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) in a heart-stopping Super Over finish.

Death Bowling Masterclass

After both teams tied at a mammoth 212 runs in regulation time, KKR's death bowling restricted LSG to just 9 runs in the Super Over. KKR chased it down in just four deliveries, solidifying their spot at the top of the points table.

The Whereby Meetings Manifesto: A Simple Framework for Better Meetings Every Time

At Whereby, we believe that meetings should be the exception, not the rule. Our Meetings Manifesto is a code of conduct for taking back control of your calendar.

The Manifesto Tenets

  1. No Agenda, No Meeting.
  2. If you're not adding value, you don't need to be there.
  3. Default to 25 minutes, not 60.
  4. Respect Focus Time above all else.

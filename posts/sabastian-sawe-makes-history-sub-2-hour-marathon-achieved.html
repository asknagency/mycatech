Sabastian Sawe Makes History: Sub-2 Hour Marathon Achieved

Published on 27/04/2026

The athletic world is in awe today as Kenya’s Sabastian Sawe did what was once thought biologically impossible: completing an officially sanctioned marathon in under two hours. Clocking in at an astounding 1:59:48, Sawe has firmly cemented his legacy in the pantheon of running legends.

Pushing Human Limits

Benefiting from revolutionary shoe technology and a perfect pacing strategy, Sawe maintained a blistering average pace. The event, held under optimal weather conditions, marks a paradigm shift in long-distance running.

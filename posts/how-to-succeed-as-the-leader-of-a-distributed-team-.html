How to succeed as the leader of a distributed team?

Leading a team you don't see in person every day requires a shift from 'tracking time' to 'tracking outcomes'. Trust and transparency are the primary currencies of the distributed leader.

Building High-Performance Cultures

  • Asynchronous Communication: Stop relying on 'quick calls' and start writing things down.
  • Psychological Safety: Create space for non-work chatter to build the social bonds that happen naturally in an office.

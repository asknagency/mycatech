AI-Powered Robot Defeats Elite Table Tennis Champions

Published on 27/04/2026

Artificial Intelligence has conquered chess, Go, and video games. Now, it has stepped into the physical realm with terrifying precision. A newly developed AI-powered robotic arm has consistently defeated top-ranked human players in table tennis.

Unmatched Reflexes

Utilizing predictive trajectory algorithms operating at 500 frames per second, the robot anticipates the spin, speed, and placement of the ball milliseconds after it leaves the human opponent's paddle. This marks a turning point in real-time robotic agility.

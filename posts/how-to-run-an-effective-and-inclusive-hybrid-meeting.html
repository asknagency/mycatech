Hybrid meetings are the hardest to get right. If not facilitated properly, remote participants often feel like observers rather than active participants.

Creating a Level Playing Field

  • Remote-First Facilitation: Always call on remote participants first for feedback.
  • Digital Whiteboarding: Don't use a physical whiteboard in the room; use a shared digital tool so everyone can see and edit simultaneously.
